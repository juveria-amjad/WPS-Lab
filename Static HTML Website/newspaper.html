The 5th International Conference on Smart Computing and Informatics successfully completed in virtual mode

The 5th International Conference on Smart Computing and Informatics (SCI-2021) was completed successfully in virtual mode by Vasavi College of Engineering from September 17 to September 18, 2021. This event was organized by the Computer Science & Engineering department.

The aim of the 5th International Conference on Smart Computing and Informatics (SCI-2021) was to present a unified platform for advanced and multi-disciplinary research towards the design of smart computing and informatics. The theme was on a broader front focuses on various innovation paradigms in system knowledge, intelligence and sustainability that may be applied to provide realistic solutions to varied problems in society, environment and industries. The scope was also extended towards the deployment of emerging computational and knowledge transfer approaches, optimizing solutions in various disciplines of science, technology and healthcare. The conference proceeding aims the publication of quality and unpublished work.

The proceedings of SCI-2021 will be published in Springer-Smart Innovation, Systems and Technologies (SIST) Series indexed in SCOPUS.

Vasavi's Security Team to manage Trump's security during his visit to Hyderabad

In a shocking turn of events, we have been informed that the security gaurds of Vasavi College of Engineering have been contacted by the United States National Security Council itself to lead the security team while Trump visits the college to inaguarate the newly created Computer Science and Engineering (AI & ML) branch. The security team of the college is reputed to be one of the best in Telangana. Therefore Trump's security team concluded to let the college manage his security. When asked how they feel working with Trump they replied, "He must have an ID card".

The newly created branched is already in news for giving tough competition to its counterparts. The IITs, NITs and IIITs are concerned as they expect this years admissions to drop by a significant margin. The inaguaration ceremony will be held on 1st of October.

BREAKING! College Canteen found to be EMPTY!!

The famous 'Canteen' which was well known to be the hub of the college was found empty. There were neither students nor food to be reported on the site. The canteen administrator said The Vasavi Times that stocking up food would be a loss for him in the current situation and that he hopes that everything would soon go back to normal. The students and faculty are reported to miss the college canteen much.
